Biography

I am Naomi Smorenburg. A 25 year old student currently finishing my Master Methodology & Statistics at Utrecht University. I also did my bachelor at Utrecht University, Psychology, with a specilization in neurobiological and cognitive psychology.

Interests

I've always been fascinated with the way the world works. As a child, my favourite tv program was ‘klokhuis’ which is an informative program in which very diverse topics are explained. This also lead to my nickname ‘Wiki’ (from Wikipedia) since I know so many useful (and useless) facts. This interests but sometimes also bores my friends and family.

Since I was always so broadly interested choosing a study was not an easy job. I finally decided to go for a direction that was not ‘beta’, ‘alfa’ but somewhat in between: ‘gamma’ - psychology. Since I was always quite strong in mathematics I thought that might be useful in statistics - that is part of all social sciences. Four years later I actually chose to do a research master entirely devoted to statistics.

Personal

I was born in the small city of Elspeet right in the middle of the Veluwe. We always had a lot of animals and people used to ask if I my parents were farmers (they are not, in fact). My parents just had a love for, especially horses, and moved to the countryside because of them. Growing up on the countryside had the big advantage we would spent most our time playing outdoors, and I spent a lot of time at our neighbours, who were actually real farmers. When I was around 8 years old I got my very first pony, a black shetland pony called Famke. Although Famke was not a huge success after all, also I was (and am) infected with the horse virus. Today I am the proud owner of my very first (real) horse: Royelle.
